About Heartlight

In a realm beyond time and space, the council of deities governs various aspects of human nature, each deity embodying a unique virtue. Troubled by the increasing suffering and cruelty on Earth, the "Lord of Compassion and Kindness" decides to take action. Observing the pain inflicted upon innocents, this benevolent deity createsHeartlight, a radiant superhero imbued with the purest elements of love, empathy, and healing.

Heartlight is dedicated to rescuing victims of abuse, providing comfort and healing, and tirelessly promoting a culture of empathy and compassion. Through their noble mission, Heartlight strives to restore hope and dignity to those in need, working towards a more compassionate and just world.
